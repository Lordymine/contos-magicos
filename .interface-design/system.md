## Direction & Feel
**Domain:** Children's Storytelling, Magic, Imagination, Warmth.
**Metaphor:** A magical storybook come to life.
**Keywords:** Warm, Soft, Playful, Safe, Magical, Tactile.

## Color World
- **Background:** Warm Cream / Parchment (`#FFFDF7`) - NOT cool white.
- **Foreground:** Dark Wood / Coffee (`#4A3B32`) - NOT black or slate.
- **Primary:** Magic Violet (`#8B5CF6`) - For creation and primary actions.
- **Secondary:** Golden Amber (`#F59E0B`) - For stars, success, rewards.
- **Accent:** Playful Pink (`#F472B6`) - For highlights and delight.
- **Surfaces:** Pure White (`#FFFFFF`) cards on Cream background.

## Signature Elements
1.  **The Floating Magic Card:**
    - Rounded corners (`2rem` / `32px`).
    - Subtle border (`border-white` or `border-primary/20`).
    - Soft shadow that grows on hover (`shadow-primary/5` -> `shadow-primary/20`).
    - Slight lift on hover (`-translate-y-1`).

2.  **The Magic Pill Navigation:**
    - Floating header, detached from the top.
    - Glassmorphism (`backdrop-blur`).
    - Rounded full (`rounded-full`).

## Design Tokens

### Spacing
- Base unit: `4px`
- Layout padding: `px-4 py-8` (Mobile), `p-8` (Cards)
- Radius: `1.5rem` (24px) for inputs/buttons, `2rem` (32px) for cards.

### Typography
- **Font:** Nunito (Rounded, friendly).
- **Headings:** Bold/ExtraBold, tight tracking.
- **Body:** Medium weight for better readability for kids/parents.

### Depth & Elevation
- **Strategy:** Layered Shadows + Soft Borders.
- **Card:** `border-2 border-white shadow-sm`.
- **Hover:** `border-primary/50 shadow-xl`.

## Component Patterns

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Violet, rounded-xl, shadow-lg, transform on hover.
- **Secondary:** Amber/Pink, used for "fun" actions.
- **Ghost:** Text only, hover with light background.

### Inputs
- **Style:** Big, chunky, friendly.
- **Height:** `h-12` or `h-14`.
- **Border:** `border-2` (not 1px), clearly visible but soft color.
- **Focus:** `ring-4` (wide soft glow).

### Story Card
- Looks like a physical book cover.
- Content clipped inside.
- Gradient spine or top bar (`from-primary to-secondary`).
